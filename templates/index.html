About CardioHealth Pro

Our mission is to make cardiovascular risk assessment accessible, accurate, and actionable for everyone.

CardioHealth Pro was developed by a team of cardiologists, data scientists, and health technology experts. Our assessment tool is based on established cardiovascular risk prediction models including the Framingham Risk Score and ACC/AHA guidelines, ensuring medical accuracy and reliability.

We believe that everyone should have access to tools that help them understand and improve their heart health. Our platform provides evidence-based insights while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data security.
